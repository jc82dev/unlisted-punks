FAQs

How much does it cost to mint a new punk?
We apply a bonding curve to the price, so each minted punk is more expensive than the previous one. Add items to the cart and check the current price.
Is my punk unique?
Yes. Once a punk is minted, no one can mint the same punk.
Can I sell my punks on Open Sea?
Yes. When you mint a new punk, it will be immediatly listed in our Open Sea collection
