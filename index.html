FAQs

What is Unlisted Punks?
Unlisted Punks is a tribute to the original CryptoPunks collection. A project that pushes the limits of what can be achieved with the NFT standard. We want to be part of the exciting history of the early years of NFTs.
How much does it cost to mint a new punk?
We apply a bonding curve to the price. The bonding curve ensures that each minted punk is more expensive than the previous one. Add items to the cart and check the current price.
Is my punk unique?
Yes. Once a punk is minted, no one can mint the same punk.
Can I sell my punks in the secondary market?
Yes. You can list them for sale in our Open Sea Collection (Ethereum/Polygon) or any other NFT marketplace (Rarible, Mintable, Binance NFT, etc)
Can I have the same punk in multiple networks?
No. Minting the same punk in different networks is not allowed.
